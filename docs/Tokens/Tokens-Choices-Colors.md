## Index convention

| Stem | Luminance | Font-Weight Equivalence |
| :--- | :--- | --- |
| `100` | Y ≃ 90 | Extra Light |
| `200` | Y ≃ 80 | Light / Thin |
| `300` | Y ≃ 70 | Book / Demi |
| `400` | Y ≃ 60 | Regular |
| `500` | Y ≃ 50 | Medium |
| `600` | Y ≃ 40 | Semibold / Demibold |
| `700` | Y ≃ 30 | Bold |
| `800` | Y ≃ 20 | Black / Extra Bold / Heavy |
| `900` | Y ≃ 10 | Extra or Ultra Black / Fat / Poster |
| > 900 | darks | "Jumbo" domain |
